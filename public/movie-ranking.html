MY BEST ANIME MOVIES OF ALL TIME

MY TOP 3 ANIME MOVIES

  1. Spirited Away (2001)

    2. This critically acclaimed film follows the story of a young girl named Chihiro, who stumbles upon a mysterious and magical world after her family accidentally enters an abandoned amusement park. When her parents are turned into pigs by a witch named Yubaba, Chihiro must work in a bathhouse for spirits to find a way to free them and return to the human world. Throughout her journey, she encounters strange creatures and learns valuable life lessons about courage, friendship, and self-discovery.

  2. Akira (1988)

    3. Set in a dystopian, post-apocalyptic Tokyo in 2019, Akira tells the story of Kaneda, the leader of a biker gang, and his friend Tetsuo, who gains immense and destructive psychic powers after a mysterious accident. As Tetsuo's powers spiral out of control, it draws the attention of the government, leading to chaos and a conspiracy centered around a powerful being named Akira. Kaneda must confront both the military and his increasingly unstable friend to save the city from destruction.

  3. Your Name (2016)

    4. The story revolves around two teenagers, Mitsuha, a girl living in rural Japan, and Taki, a boy in Tokyo, who mysteriously begin to swap bodies. As they navigate each other's lives, they form a deep connection despite never having met in person. The film takes an emotional twist when Taki discovers that the two of them are living in different time periods, and he must race against time to save Mitsuha and her town from a looming disaster.
